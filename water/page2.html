The following description may be considered "science-ey", but we have done our best to break it down in ways that anyone can understand. Water at the molecular level is made up of two elements: Hydrogen and Oxygen. Both of which are found abundantly all over the world, especially in the air that you breath! The small bottle-sized hose that you carry uses a process called alchemy to pull in hydrogen and oxygen atoms from your surroundings, and combine them in appropriate ways to create water! This process is continually done so that you can spend as long as you need taking care of your lawn, plants, or any other accomplishments you feel you can complete using Wireless Water!
